# AI recruitment for enterprises

This domain collects modern patterns for applying AI across enterprise talent
acquisition, from finding candidates to running fairer interviews, without
tripping over the bias, privacy and regulatory constraints that make hiring a
high-stakes use of AI.

## Where to start

- Finding and ranking talent: [[AI sourcing and candidate matching]].
- Doing it lawfully and fairly: [[Bias audits and hiring compliance]].
- Helping interviewers, not replacing them: [[Structured interview copilots]].
- Protecting applicants' data: [[Candidate data privacy and consent]].

## The through-line

Hiring decisions affect people's livelihoods and are heavily regulated, so every
pattern here is built around explainability, human decision ownership, and an
auditable record. AI accelerates the funnel; it does not get to make the call
unchecked.
