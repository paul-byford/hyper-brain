# Welcome to Hyper Brain

Hyper Brain is your company's memory: connected, current, and scoped to what you
are allowed to see. This page is part of the **commons**, the shared space every
signed-in person can read, so it is the first thing a new joiner lands on.

## What you can do here

- **Ask questions.** Use search and answer to retrieve across every domain you
  have been granted, with citations and an honest account of any gaps.
- **Keep personal notes.** Everything you write with add_note lands in your own
  private personal space. Only you can see it until you choose to share it.
- **Share when you are ready.** Share a whole personal domain, or a single note,
  with a colleague or a group. Revoke it at any time. What you never share stays
  private for good.

## How access works

Access is granted by domain. Your teams and the commons come from the company
policy; your personal space is yours automatically; and anything shared with you
shows up alongside your own domains. A caller only ever retrieves from the
sub-graph they may read. For the precise definitions, see the [[Glossary]] and
[[Security and privacy basics]].
