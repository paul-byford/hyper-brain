# Glossary

The vocabulary the rest of the commons uses. Start from
[[Welcome to Hyper Brain]] or [[Getting started with the brain]] for the tour; this
page is the quick reference.

## Terms

- **Commons.** The shared domain every signed-in person can read. Getting started,
  the [[Company handbook]], and anything meant for everyone.
- **Personal space.** A private domain, one per person, that only its owner can read
  until they share it.
- **Team domain.** A domain granted to a team by policy, an isolation boundary that
  only its members retrieve from.
- **Grant.** An entry in the access policy that widens what an identity may read (or
  write). Grants never open a team domain to everyone.
- **Wikilink.** A `[[Title]]` reference from one document to another in the same
  domain. Wikilinks build the link graph; see [[Writing good notes]].
- **Proposal and review.** A drafted document waiting for a human to approve it into
  a team domain, so nothing lands live unreviewed.
- **Grounded answer.** A composed answer that cites the retrieved passages behind it
  and states any gaps, rather than guessing.
- **Isolation.** The guarantee that retrieval, and even link expansion, stays inside
  the domains you may read. See [[Security and privacy basics]].
