# Company handbook

The handbook is the shared, always-current reference every colleague can read. It
lives in the commons so a new joiner has real material to explore on day one,
alongside [[Getting started with the brain]].

## How knowledge is organised

Knowledge is grouped into domains. The commons is for everyone; each team has its
own domain; and every person has a private personal space. You retrieve across the
domains you have been granted, and only those. The [[Glossary]] defines each of
these terms precisely.

## Finding your way around

Search is the front door. Ask in plain language, read the grounded answer, and
follow its citations into the underlying documents. When you learn something worth
keeping, capture it as a note so it is there next time.

## Working safely

Access is least-privilege by default and everything is scoped to your identity. For
what that means in practice, and how sharing changes it, read
[[Security and privacy basics]].
