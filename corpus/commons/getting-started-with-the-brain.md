# Getting started with the brain

New here? This is the short path from signing in to getting value. It builds on
[[Welcome to Hyper Brain]] and points you at the rest of the commons.

## Ask a question

Type a question into search. The brain retrieves across every domain you have been
granted, ranks the best passages, and can compose a grounded answer with citations
and an honest note of any gaps. Start broad, then follow the citations into the
documents behind an answer.

## Keep your own notes

Anything you capture lands in your private personal space, visible only to you. The
more you write, the more the brain can retrieve for you later. For how to structure
a note so it is easy to find and connect, see [[Writing good notes]].

## Share when you are ready

Nothing you write is shared until you choose to share it. You can hand a single note
or a whole space to a colleague and revoke it at any time. See
[[Sharing and collaboration]] for the details.

## Where things live

Your teams and the commons come from company policy; your personal space is yours
automatically. For the vocabulary, keep the [[Glossary]] handy.
